Person.

They say the universe is vast and impossible to measure, but I have realized that the universe isn't just stars and galaxies. Sometimes, the universe is just a person who makes the world feel a little bit more balanced. You are that person for me.

I know the reality. I am 21 and you are 30 plus. I respect the woman you have become. I admire your elegance and your strength. I am not here to move too fast or to pretend; I just want to be the one who brings a smile to your face.

You aren't just a friend; you have become my favorite person. I want to be in your corner, celebrating your wins and being a steady presence in your life. I am giving you this little piece of my world today just to see you smile.

Accept to be my favorite person forever?
